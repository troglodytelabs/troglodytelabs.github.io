About the studio

Troglodyte Labs is a one-person studio by Devin Dyson, focused on small, durable software. I design, prototype, and ship products end-to-end with a bias toward clarity, speed, and long-term maintainability.

Philosophy

  • Scope ruthlessly; ship early, iterate in public.
  • Favor simplicity over frameworks and ceremony.
  • Make opinions visible; reduce user decision fatigue.
  • Keep the stack boring, the product sharp.

Capabilities

  • Product design (UX, UI, copy)
  • Web apps (Python/Django, TypeScript/React)
  • Data & analytics (Postgres, basic ML)
  • Brand systems & lightweight marketing sites

Stacks I reach for

  • Django, DRF, Celery
  • Postgres, Redis
  • React, Next.js (when needed), Tailwind
  • Deploy: Fly.io / Render / Docker on VPS

Currently

  • Iterating on Calstack scheduling UX
  • Expanding Moodlog journaling & reports
  • New Plotflow AxiDraw series
